Kontrolltårn helps Norwegian hospitals plan operating rooms, patients, staff, and equipment together. Its algorithms propose plans six to eight weeks ahead while letting coordinators adjust them as circumstances change.

The application gives coordinators and clinical leaders a shared view of capacity and demand. It runs on ServiceNow and integrates with hospital systems including DIPS and GAT, with sensitive patient data kept inside a secure platform.

## My part

I’m one of the central developers on Kontrolltårn, working on the application and the technical foundations that connect planning, clinical data, and day-to-day use.

The product is developed by KPMG in close collaboration with Oslo University Hospital, with NEO Consulting also part of the project.
