<p class="about-lede">I’m a multidisciplinary software engineer, designer, and technical leader with more than 20 years of experience building, modernizing, and occasionally coercing complex systems into doing useful things.</p>

My work has moved through interactive exhibitions, games, creative developer tools, cross-platform applications, enterprise integrations, and secure public-sector software. The common thread is turning difficult technical territory into practical systems that people can understand, extend, and rely on.

I love language, expression, and collaboration, and I’m proud to call myself a forever amateur—the work of self-improvement is never complete. On this site you may find musings, strong opinions, video games, music, enterprise software, and anything and everything in between.

## Selected work

- Led development of an interactive, real-time view into secure healthcare planning data for the Norwegian public sector.
- Built safety and location-tracking companion applications for security personnel working in the field.
- Created developer tooling that lets web developers contribute TypeScript and React frontends to ServiceNow projects without deep platform specialization.
- Helped build Fuse’s cross-platform UX toolchain and worked across C#, C++, Objective-C, Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, Uno, and .UX.
- Designed visual programming and particle-system tools that enabled non-technical authors to create interactive content.
- Integrated sensor networks, computer vision, and high-performance graphics into installations for museums and cultural institutions.
- Built a networked, 20-plus-player exhibit teaching diplomacy and collaborative problem-solving through a shared simulated crisis.

## Speaking and open source

I created **DConsole**, an extensible developer console that brought real-time introspection and debugging to Flash and AIR projects. I’ve contributed to the Haxe ecosystem, presented three sessions at **Flash on the Beach** and **Reasons to be Creative** in Brighton, and delivered international product demonstrations and workshops for Fuse.
